How to use:
Replace {{COURSE_TITLE}} with your course name (e.g. CSS Fundamentals)

Replace {{STORAGE_KEY}} with a unique key (e.g. cssFundamentalsProgress)

Duplicate the .video-item block and update:

for attribute and id on checkbox (e.g., video-1, video-2, ...)

Video title text inside .video-title

src YouTube embed URL with your video ID

Description text inside .video-description

This way, you keep the page consistent and just swap the content!
